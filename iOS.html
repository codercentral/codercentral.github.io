Instructor Background

Boris is a senior at Amador Valley High School. He is a co-president and founder of Competitive Computer Science Club, a club at Amador that encourages competition among high school students by hosting monthly programming contests. This past summer he went to Make School, an educational start-up in San Francisco that teaches students iOS app and game development. He now has his own iOS game, Bouncee Blob, on the app store, and is eager to pass the knowledge he learned from Make School on to the next generation of programmers. In his free time, Boris enjoys playing the piano, watching TV, and playing basketball.
