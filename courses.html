Unsure of where to start? Explore our tracks!
You are at Coder Central- the start of your coding trip! It's the connecting station for three major tracks: Robotics, AP Computer Science, and Web Development. Look at the map below to help you decide which track you want to travel on. Keep in mind, the conductors are super friendly so don't be afraid to approach them with questions. Don't worry if you've never explored the coding world, that's what we're here for!

Robotics
Can you hear the sounds of motors? They're coming from robots made by other Coder Central commuters! On this track, you can jump on the either the C++ or Python train. The Python train will take you to the Town of Raspberry Pi, a fun way to get introduced to our robot friends. The C++ train will wisk you away to the grand City of Arduino to see Arduinites!
AP Computer Science
This is the perfect track for the high school commuter who are about to board onto the Java train. It stops by important stations such as Computing with Algorithms which is essential for students to do well on the AP Computer Science course. Furthermore, students can stop by the Bootcamp station to get a great review right before the AP Computer Science test.
Web Design
For this track, you will be traveling in a three car train: HTML, CSS, and Javascript. While on this track, the conductor will give you opportunities to build websites for small businesses around the community - a great way to beef up your resume. By the time you reach the final station, you will find yourself as one of the 1% of Americans who know how to build websites!
