Step 3: Learn About the Coder Central Package

Our Philosophies

Teach

  • Instructors are more than teachers, they are life and academic mentors
  • Discussion sections where students learn to work in a group and teach one another
  • Problems that help students learn by doing

Inspire

  • First 15 minutes of every class is a creative lab where students are introduced to a new topic ranging from sports, politics, to science and think of cool and innovative ways of apps they can build to solve real world problems
  • Giving students the tools to discover coding on their own as well as helping them realize the purpose of why they are learning programming

Apply

  • Some courses include cool internship opportunities from our web design services
  • All courses include internship opportunities on our content development team to edit and test our developing content to help us inspire more students to solve real world problems!
