Introduction to Programming
Want to learn your first programming language but don't know where to start? Or have you heard of the many benefits that come with learning Python? Then this is the course for you! The instructor will teach you the fundamentals of Python, equipping you with an incredibly empowering skill, possibly changing how you look at the world. Use the skills you learned to create all sorts of programs for your day to day lives.

Course Overview

1 Expressions, Variables, Input/Output
2 Basic Data Structures
3 Conditionals and Control Flow
4 Loops and Iteration
5 Turtle Graphics
6 Object-Oriented Programming
7 Functions and Scope
8 Classes and Objects
9 Working with Files
10 Final Project and Student Showcase

Instructor

Sidd Desai is currently an undergraduate majoring in Computer Science at UCI. With regards to programming, he is well-versed in Python, C++, and Java with some HTML, CSS, and Javascript knowledge. He is passionate about learning technology and teaching what he has learned. Sidd graduated Amador Valley High School in 2014. He was ACE Coding’s first Branch Director of the Pleasanton Library and taught a C++ workshop in conjunction with Microsoft at ACE Code Day. In high school, he was a member of the Robotics Club’s software division and also tutored students at Summit Tutoring Center. In his free time he enjoys b-boying, riding his bike, and watching anime.
