Why Coder Central?}

Learn. Inspired. Create.
At Coder Central, our courses does not just include lessons with our instructors, but also many other activities and resources as well to help our students learn, inspire, and create. We strive to create an environment the replicates how professionals collaboratively learn on the job. We also heavily emphasize practical application of the skills we teach so students are given internship opportunities at Coder Central with our Content Development Team and Web Services Team. Take a look below to see all the the other things that are included when you sign up for one of our classes.

What's included in our classes?

Challenge Sets

After every class, we provide problems and challenges for students to practice and apply skills they learned in class. Written by Coder Central instructors and content creation team, our challenge sets include both review problems and harder challenges that push and develop students' problem solving and critical thinking skills. Solutions including a written guide and video walkthroughs are provided after the set is due the following class.

Reference Sheets

For each topic, reference sheets to online guides and forums are provided to students to review and reinforce the material covered in class. Through these reference sheets, students gain self sufficiency and become a more independent coder. This is a skill we heavily emphasize because with coding standards changing on a monthly basis, it is cruicial that students have the skills to keep up with them even after the class.

Discussion Sessions

We also place heavy emphasis on teamwork and collaboration in our courses because this is a skill that coders must have to be able to work in the industry. To reflect this, we hold an online discussion session between classes for students. These are moderated by an instructor or teaching assistant. During this time, students work together to learn and review concepts covered in class and in the homework, and teach each other on how to work through the harder problems.

Interactive Videos

Throughout our courses, students build several projects to apply their knowledge and create something fun, practical, and ultimately that shows how coding can solve real world problems. Our instructors create and release interactive videos to help students get started and guide them through the first steps of the project.

Education for the 21st Century

What does it take to be a productive member of today's economy? A college degree? A master's degree? Or perhaps an excellent GPA from high school and college? The reality is, it takes more than just a prestigious degree or a stellar GPA. More than ever now, companies are looking for creative people with a drive to solve problems- qualities that are not taught in school.

If anything, what we see in school today is doing the exact opposite. Students are conditioned to fear failure as it comes with associated punishments such as a bad grade or peer judgement. This not only discourages students to continue to try, but also destroys their creative potentials.

Creativity is born from mistakes and learning from them. At Coder Central, courses are revolved around reviving the innate creativity and curiosity that people are born with. Small class sizes ensures that students receive personalized attention and for them to feel like they are in a safe environment. Teachers at Coder Central place a heavy emphasis on teaching student because they want to learn as opposed to telling them what they should learn.

Curriculums are designed with the intention of challenging students and fostering their ability and motivation to learn on their own. This is something that we believe is far more valuable than just telling students what they should know.
