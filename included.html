At Coder Central, our course does not just include lessons with our instructors, but also many other activities and resources as well to help our students learn, inspire, and create. Take a look below to see all the the other things that are included when you sign up for one of our classes.

What's included in our classes?

Challenge Sets

After every class, we provide problems and challenges for students to practice and apply skills they learned in class. Written by Coder Central instructors and content creation team, our challenge sets include both review problems and harder challenges that push and develop students' problem solving and critical thinking skills. Solutions including a written guide and video walkthroughs are provided after the set is due the following class.

Reference Sheets

For each topic, reference sheets to online guides and forums are provided to students to review and reinforce the material covered in class. Through these reference sheets, students gain self sufficiency and become a more independent coder. This is a skill we heavily emphasize because with coding standards changing on a monthly basis, it is cruicial that students have the skills to keep up with them even after the class.

Discussion Sessions

We also place heavy emphasis on teamwork and collaboration in our courses because this is a skill that coders must have to be able to work in the industry. To reflect this, we hold an online discussion session between classes for students. These are moderated by an instructor or teaching assistant. During this time, students work together to learn and review concepts covered in class and in the homework, and teach each other on how to work through the harder problems.

Interactive Videos

Throughout our courses, students build several projects to apply their knowledge and create something fun, practical, and ultimately that shows how coding can solve real world problems. Our instructors create and release interactive videos to help students get started and guide them through the first steps of the project.
