Students teaching students.
In the summer of 2015, Daniel Huang gathered us together to form Coder Central. We provide in-person and -in the near future- online coding courses in Pleasanton, CA. All content is especially created by our content development team including easy to consume videos, informative and concise packets, and many practice problems that will help students understand key concepts. During our classes, we assign students real projects that directly help small businesses in the local community, and we even offer our students opportunities to work for us! Most importantly, we truely want to inspire students to love learning again because we realize our education system is killing this fundamental human passion.
