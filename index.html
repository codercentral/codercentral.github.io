Interdisciplinary Coding Courses

Founded by Daniel Huang in the summer of 2015,

Coder Central strives to excite students grades 5th - 12th to love learning again. Technology now is automating more and more jobs. From writing business reports, to call centers, and even driving. This means we need our students to be more innovative problem solvers instead of test-takers.

We need students to not fear asking and answering questions. Because that is how innovation happens. Through our interdisciplinary coding courses, we want to revive each student's innate curiosity to motivate them to push their personal boundaries of what is possible.

Designed by current business, computer science, and animation students

at UCLA, UC Irvine, and Cal Arts, Coder Central courses are meant to be inter-disciplinary. They combine traditional school subjects such as English, Math, and Science with coding!

Created by high achieving students at Amador Valley High School, projects were designed with Common Core curriculum standards in mind. Assign projects often reflect on real world problems, and are meant to challenge students grades 5th - 12th of all economic backgrounds.

We Are Partnered With

We believe education should be affordable for all students.

Thus for any course that we offer, we allow you to pay us what you want! Through our research, we have found that for comparable coding course in the Bay Area, the average cost is around $700. While we do not ask for this amount, please keep it in mind throughout the course.
After the course is over, we meet with you and your student together to assess his or her progress and overall satisfaction with the course. Through this meeting, we trust that your payment for our course reflects your satisfaction and your ability to pay.
