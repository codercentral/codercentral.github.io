Introduction to Programming
As part of the Robotics Track, you will learn the fundamental C++ tools and logic through this course taught by Evan McNeil, a programmer on Amador Valley High School's prestigious AVBotz team. This class is great for robotic enthusiasts or just the casual programmer wanting to pick up C++.

Course Overview

1 Variables, Expressions, and Input/Output
2 Basic Data Structures
3 Conditionals and Control Flow
4 Loops
5 Review and In-Class Project
6 Introduction to Functions
7 Classes and Objects: Part I
8 Classes and Objects: Part II
9 Practice with Functions and Objects, Final Project Assigned
10 Project Finishing Touches and Student Showcase

Instructor Background

Evan is a junior at Amador Valley High School. He is one of the main software developers on AVHS's prestigious AVBots team where they engineer, program, and build an autonomous submarine to compete at an international collegiate robotics competition. He has been an instructor at ACE Coding for the past year and is currently the Director of Content at the Harvest Park branch of ACE Coding. He is also experienced in several programming languages including Python, C++, BASIC, C#, C, and HTML/CSS/JavaScript.
