What can you do?
What matters more when you're applying for a job: a 4.0 GPA or previous work experience. Now more than ever, companies care more about what you do outside of the classroom rather than inside the classroom. At Coder Central, we offer internship opportunities for middle and high school students in marketing, web design, and content development. All of these internships place emphasis on providing real world working experience to inspire students to think beyond the classroom as well as helping them in applying for a job. If you're interested in just coding, we are also planning on creating Hack Clubs and Competition Programming Clubs in the near future so stay tuned! Also, if you have any questions regarding these programs, please let us know at info@codercentral.org.

Web Development Services

Build real websites for real local business

As more and more small business rely on websites to conduct their business, it's more important than ever that they have a reliable and professional website. And Coder Central offers affordable web design services lead by experienced web designer Daniel Huang and made by Coder Central interns and students. While taking our web design course(s), students will automatically be admitted into this branch of Coder Central. Otherwise, interested students need to go through a rigorous application and interview process.

*Note: mandatory monthly meetings will be held online. In person meetings will be held at the local library(ies)

Web Programmer Graphics Designers
We're looking for young up-and-coming web programmers! You will be building assigned pages of a website for local small businesses. You will be working closely with the graphics design department, web design courses, as well as Daniel- the director of Coder Central's Web Services branch. While there are no clients, the time commitment will be low (aka, nothing), but when there are clients, expect to commit around 10 to 15 hours of work per week as our clients need their websites up as soon as possible. No website can look great without graphics designers! As a graphics designer, you will be designing all of the graphics and artwork that goes into our clients websites. This involves working closely with the clients to understand what they need and how you can help them achieve their ideal website. Along with the clients themselves, you will be working with the web programmers from the web design courses, web programmer interns, as well as Daniel- the director of Coder Central's Web Services branch. For each client, expect around a 10 to 15 hours of work a week as our clients need their sites up as soon as possible.
What are we looking for?
  • Middle/High School students
  • Sufficient knowledge of HTML CSS
  • Have taken/is taking a web design course
  • Creative drive to build web applications
  • Attention to details
  • Responsibility
  • Interpersonal/Cooperation skills
  • An open mind to learning!
 What are we looking for?
  • Middle/High School students
  • Passionate artist
  • Have 2-3 art/graphics disign projects in portfolio
  • Basic Photoshop/Adobe Illustrator skills
  • Attention to details
  • Responsibility
  • Interpersonal/Cooperation skills

Youth Commission | Marketing

Help spread Coder Central's mission!

Coder Central's Youth Commission is a special branch created just for middle and high school students. It is mentored and directed by Coder Central's marketing department and aims to raise over $10,000 before next summer to help Coder Central provide coding classes to low-income areas as well as raising greater awareness of the importance of a well directed education.

*Note: mandatory monthly meetings will be held at the Pleasanton/local Library(ies)

General Marketing Online Marketing Print Marketing
Ever wanted to be a part of something bigger than yourself? As part of the Youth Commission's General Marketing department, you will be a part of spreading Coder Central's mission of inspiring students to look beyond the classroom walls. This means that you will be in charge of organizing events, fundraisers, and other promotional activities to brand Coder Central as a place where students rediscover the purpose of getting an education and to truly be curious about the world they live in. You will be working closely with Coder Central's Director of Marketing- Cherri, as well as the print and online marketing teams on Youth Commission. You will also get the opportunity to reach out and network with influential figures in tech, education, and more! The time commitment for this internship is around 6 to 8 hours a week. What is cheapest form of marketing? Online marketing! You will be maintaining all of Coder Central's social media pages through Hootsuite as well as increasing Coder Central's online presence. In this department, you will also be in charge of writing or assigning weekly blogs that will general web traffic to this very website! Furthermore, you will get the opportunity to work with Coder Central's Director of Marketing- Cherri and realize the importance of SEO (Search Engine Optimization) as well as Youth Commission's general and print marketing departments. The time commitment for this internship is around 6 to 8 hours a week. If you love to draw and create cool designs, Youth Commission needs you! As part of the Print department at Youth Commission, you will be artistically branding Coder Central's image to the public. Example projects you will be completing include: flyers, banners, postcards, stickers, and more. You will be working closely with Coder Central's Director of Marketing- Cherri as well as the general and online marketing teams on Youth Commision. Expect a time commitment around 6 to 8 hours a week.

Content Development | Editor

Improve the learning experience at Coder Central

This is one of Coder Central's core teams that is in charge of developing content for all of our classes and workshops. This includes creating interactive videos, supplemental packets, and engaging problems for our students to hone their programming skills.

*Note: mandatory monthly meetings will be held at the Pleasanton/local Library(ies)

Editor
Coder Central aims to inspire hundreds of students. This means that hundreds of students will be using our content which is why we need you to help make it better! As an editor for Coder Central's Content Development team, you will be beta-testing all of our content which includes: packets, videos, and problems. You will also help our content developers' syntax and grammar. You will be working closely with Coder Central's Director of Content Development- Jerry, as well as the rest of the Content Development and Instructors team. The time commitment will be around 5 to 8 hours a week.
What are we looking for?
  • Middle/High School students
  • Strong grammar skills
  • Passion for learning
  • Strong communication skills
  • Attention to details
  • Responsibility
  • Interpersonal/Cooperation skills
  • An open mind to learning!
