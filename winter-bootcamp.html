Personalized Education

Our bootcamp, designed for 5th - 12th graders, simluate the format of college courses. Lessons are taught by the instructor to the whole class, and smaller workshop sections led by TA's will clarify and work through any questions, and facilitate problem solving sessions.

Project Based Curriculum

We emphasize having students learn through hands-on projects and problem sets. By the end of the bootcamp, they will be programming their own game (with graphics and animation!) to present!

Pay What You Want!

Our Payment Model

Bootcamps at Coder Central don't work like normal courses - you get to pay what you want when the whole course is over! We do ask for a $50 deposit when registering, however, to confirm your attendance for the whole bootcamp.

How it Works

Discuss with your child after the course is over and evaluate our bootcamp based on his or her experience and your overall impressions, and pay us tuition accordingly. All that we ask is that you be fair if your child enjoyed and learned a lot from our course - most other bootcamps of this length charge around $350 to $450.
