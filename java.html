Introduction to Programming
Want to prepare for the AP Computer Science course, but don't have any coding experience? As a former AP Computer Science student, Maitri will use actual AP Computer Science projects from Amador Valley High School as well as real problems from the AP Computer Science Test. It will engage students to think beyond the AP curriculum to create their own projects and truly master the material.
